D'SANDWICHES HISTORY

D'Sandwiches was founded in 2016 by Anisah Binti Jamal in Johor Bahru, Johor. Since its establishment, the brand has become well-known for serving delicious and high-quality sandwiches, offering a variety of options to satisfy different tastes. Anisah's passion for creating the perfect sandwich has driven the success of D'Sandwiches, making it a beloved choice for many in the local community!
